DISSY 2024

Dissy, or ‘Brandon Meyers,’ was born in 2003 in New York and develops projects in his studio in Connecticut.

Films and real-world experience heavily inspire Dissy. He captures these elements within his pieces as he presents a unique perspective of society.

In 2024, Dissy founded POTENCY™ to study and document the explicit truth of real-world situations with curated prints and selected garments. POTENCY™ reveals society’s potent behaviors and functionality.

To contact Dissy visit the contact page or send an email to dissy@potencyus.com
